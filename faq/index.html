Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

Our peaceful bungolos are on the shores of Lake Geneva, Wisconsin. For a map to the retreat, please see our About section.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

The Unplugged retreat is an amazing experience for all teams that are ready to unplug from typical distractions and focus on team building.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Our retreat is meant for small teams, as we have 12 private rooms.

Is there really no wifi available?

We are proud to be wifi free! However, we do have a computer room available if needed.
